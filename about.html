A Legacy of Excellence

50 Years in the Hing Industry

SKAK International stands as a beacon of excellence in the world of spices, boasting a rich legacy spanning five decades. Established with a commitment to importing and manufacturing Hing from various corners of the globe, this legendary company has been a cornerstone of quality and innovation.

Our story begins in pre-partition India, at Dera Ismail Khan, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, a region in current day Pakistan, where centuries of tradition meets modern expertise.

🏛️
Seeds of a long journey, rooted in tradition, flourishing through generations

— The SKAK International Story

Our Journey Through Time

The Foundation

Chetan Das Bhatia (1903 - 1972) is born in the idyllic settings of Dera Ismail Khan. His life soon becomes infused with the sharp, piercing aroma of Hing. By his youth, Lala Ji had developed close bonds with the local Pashtuns, the area's major Hing producers.

As the spice was in high demand in mainland India, Lala Ji soon began procuring Hing from the Pashtuns and trading it with Hindus in mainland India. This would mark the beginning of a legacy that runs to this day.

1903

Pre-Partition Success

Lala Ji's endearing persona made him the first face of SKAK International, as the company transported high quality Hing into pre-partition mainland India. His deep relationships with Pashtun producers became the foundation of our quality sourcing.

The region had been a hub for cultivating high quality Hing since centuries, populated by both Pashtun and Hindokwan (Hindu) communities working in harmony.

1920s-1940s

Second Generation Leadership

Ram Lal Bhatia (1931-2010) took over the reins of the family business, consolidating SKAK International's Hing business in India. He focused primarily on the Hindi heartland of India's north, expanding the company's reach and establishing it as a trusted name in the Indian spice market.

After partition, the business adapted to new challenges while maintaining the same commitment to quality. Ram Lal Bhatia continued to build upon his father's legacy, ensuring the family's expertise in hing sourcing and trading remained unmatched.

1931

Third Generation Leadership

Sunil K Bhatia (1972-2020), the eldest son of Ram Lal Bhatia, joined the family business and played a crucial role in expanding the company's operations. He brought fresh perspectives and modern approaches to the traditional hing business.

Arunal Bhatia (1982-2014), the second son of Ram Lal Bhatia, also contributed significantly to the family business, working alongside his brother to strengthen SKAK International's position in the market.

1972-2014

Current Generation Leadership

The fourth generation of the Bhatia family now leads SKAK International with a perfect blend of traditional values and modern business practices. Rishab Bhatia, the eldest son of Sunil K. Bhatia, looks after purchasing operations, ensuring the highest quality standards in sourcing.

Mrinal Bhatia, the youngest in the current generation, handles both purchasing and sales operations, bringing fresh energy and innovative approaches to the business.

Kunal Bhatia focuses on sales operations, expanding the company's reach and maintaining strong relationships with customers across India. Together, they continue the legacy of excellence established by their forefathers.
