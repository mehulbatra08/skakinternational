50 Years of Legacy

SKAK International has been a pioneering figure in the Hing import business since its inception. For more than 50 years, we have been a reliable source of premium-quality Hing, serving a clientele which stretches PAN INDIA.

Our story begins in pre-partition India, at Dera Ismail Khan, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, where our founder Lala Chetan Das Bhatia established deep connections with local Pashtun producers, creating a legacy that runs to this day.

1903 - The Beginning

Lala Chetan Das Bhatia is born in Dera Ismail Khan, beginning our family's journey with hing.

1965 - Expansion

Shri Ajay Bhatia discovers the vast South Indian market, expanding our reach across the country.

1988 - New Generation

Sharad Bhatia joins at age 20, bringing fresh perspectives to the fourth generation.

Steps for Harvesting Hing

1
Identifying the Plant

During April and May, asafoetida plants emerge. Recognition is key; spotting its distinct appearance aids farmers in harvesting.

2
Extracting the Asafoetida

From mid-July to mid-September, sequential incisions on the root allow resin to seep out, yielding 30-50 kg from 3-4 thousand plants.

3
Sun Exposure

The asafoetida roots undergo 5-10 days of direct sun exposure, vital for resin preparation and flavor enhancement.

4
Storing & Packing

By September-end, the produce is brought down from mountains, checked, and repacked in export-ready containers.
